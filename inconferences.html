Venue & Location

About Dharan

Dharan is a major city in eastern Nepal, known for its diverse culture, clean environment, and status as a center of education and healthcare. Nestled between the mountains and the plains, it offers a unique blend of natural beauty and urban energy, making it an ideal location for an international gathering.

Conference Venue

The conference will be held at the Central Campus of Technology (Hatisar Campus), a premier institution in the region. Its modern facilities and academic environment provide the perfect setting for learning and collaboration.

How to Reach Dharan

Travel guidance for our international and domestic attendees.

By Air

The nearest airport is Biratnagar Airport (BIR), approximately 40km (1-hour drive) from Dharan. It is well-connected with daily flights from Kathmandu (KTM). Taxis are readily available at the airport to take you to Dharan.

By Road (Domestic)

Deluxe tourist buses and microbuses run daily from Kathmandu and other major cities like Pokhara and Chitwan to Dharan. The journey from Kathmandu takes approximately 8-10 hours. The main bus park is centrally located, with easy access to hotels.

From India (Train/Road)

The closest major Indian railway station is New Jalpaiguri (NJP). From there, you can take a taxi to the Kakarbhitta border (approx. 1-2 hours), cross, and then take a bus or taxi to Dharan (approx. 2-3 hours). Alternatively, reach Jogbani by train and cross the border to Biratnagar, which is very close to Dharan.
